About me

Research scientist, engineer, and curious observer.

I am a research scientist who enjoys tackling real-world engineering challenges. My formal education is in Aerospace Engineering, culminating in a PhD from Delft University of Technology.

Outside of work, I enjoy sports and love capturing photos with my camera. This page brings together the professional and personal sides of my life: the models I build, the questions I work on, and the moments I like to document outside the office.

What I do for work

Combining mathematical modelling with engineering insight.

I enjoy combining mathematical skills, scientific computing, and engineering insight to develop practical solutions for wind energy and offshore systems.

My work sits between research and application: turning methods into models, simulations, and decision-support tools for design, operations, maintenance, cost, reliability, and sustainability questions.
